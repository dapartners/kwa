My Cranfield Project proposal

In a small town in Douala-cameroon called "boite de singe" very close to the Douala international airport, toilet waste is being disposed of improperly. These wastes are collected from households by the means of sewage tankers for the purpose of disposal and treatment but what happens is, these are disposed into an already filled pit causing to overflow into neighbouring homes. In my opinion i think that if a biogas plant is setup in this area, it could serve as real time solution to the improper sewage disposal as well as provide employment and energy to most homes in Douala at a cheaper rate.
